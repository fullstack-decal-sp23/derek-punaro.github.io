  • CareXc

    • Completing an internship in the health industry can be a valuable experience for students interested in pursuing a career in healthcare. Derek Punaro, a student who attended UC Berkeley, had the opportunity to complete such an internship at CareXc, a company that specializes in providing digital health solutions. During his internship, Derek completed a full static website and then turned that static website into a dynamic website using applications such as Docker, Git, and React. This experience allowed Derek to gain hands-on experience in web development and deployment, as well as learn about the latest technologies and practices used in the industry. By working alongside industry professionals in a fast-paced and dynamic environment, Derek gained valuable skills and knowledge that will help him succeed in a career in healthcare technology.
